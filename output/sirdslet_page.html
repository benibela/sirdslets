AbSIRDlet

Written by Lewey Geselowitz

Controls:
Drag left mouse button to draw.
Left click on the 'height pallette' on the right to select the height to draw with.
Tap the right mouse button to toggle between SIRDS and height mode.
Refresh the page to clear the screen
